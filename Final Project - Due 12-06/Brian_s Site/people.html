Meet the Trainers

  • Brian Shultes

    • Founder/Lead Trainer

  • Erik Schulz

    • Trainer

Brian Shultes is the founder and lead trainer at Primal Fitness Group. He also leads the strength and physical conditioning curriculum for student-athletes at Saint Stephen's Episcopal School in Bradenton, FL. He has been involved in athletics and performance development since 1978, and holds two bachelor’s degrees in Physical Education and Exercise Physiology as well as a Master’s Degree in Sports Management/Sport Science. Brian is a Certified Personal Trainer, Certified Nutrition Practitioner, Strength and Conditioning Specialist, and a P.I.C.P. Level 1 and 2 Certified Coach. He has trained clients privately as the owner of LiftStrong Fitness Personal Training Studio and MobileFiT Mobile Training Studio, and spent 10 years as the women’s basketball head coach at Eckerd College.

Erik Schulz is an alum of Saint Stephen's Episcopal School and recent graduate of Florida Southern College (FSC). He holds a degree in Human Movement & Performance and is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist. At FSC, he helped design fitness programs, trained the school’s athletic teams, taught new personal trainers, and was a presenter at the Gulf Coast Fitness Expo. A former multi-sport athlete himself, Erik has a passion for helping people reach their potential through training programs designed to fit each individual. He has experience with all levels of clients and athletes.
